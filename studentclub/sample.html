ABOUT STUDENT CLUB

The Cognizant Student Club is launched to bridge the gap between the college students and the corporate. Pre-final year is the right time for enhancing skills and preparing for placement. In this regard, Cognizant student club will facilitates development of skills and competency.

OBJECTIVES OF THE STUDENT CLUB:

1.To develop the technical knowledge and skills of the students.

2.To enhance the communication and interpersonal skills of the students

3.To provide a holistic personality development.

4.To provide corporate and Industry Exposure.
